Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

1. Data We Collect

Spendly collects personal expense information that you voluntarily provide, including transaction amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We also collect account information such as your email address and authentication credentials. All data is stored securely on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide expense tracking and analysis features. This includes generating spending summaries, category breakdowns, and time-based reports. Your data is used only for these core functionalities and to improve your experience with the service.

3. Data Storage

Your expense data is stored on secure servers with industry-standard encryption. We implement reasonable security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no system can guarantee absolute security, and you are responsible for maintaining backup copies of your important data.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not sell, trade, or rent your personal financial information to third parties. We may use trusted third-party services for hosting and infrastructure, but these providers are bound by confidentiality agreements and data protection requirements.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or delete your personal data at any time through your account settings. You may also request a complete export of your data in a machine-readable format. Contact us if you need assistance with any of these actions.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at privacy@spendly.com.
